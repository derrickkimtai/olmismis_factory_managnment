About Us

The Olmismis Farmers Co-operative Society is a group of dedicated farmers who are passionate about producing high-quality coffee beans. We focus on sustainable farming practices and ensuring fair compensation for our members. We believe in providing exceptional service to our customers and delivering the finest coffee experience.

Our Services

We offer a range of coffee processing services to meet your needs. From sourcing and roasting the finest beans to grinding and packaging, we can help you create the perfect cup of coffee.

  • Coffee Bean Sourcing
  • Coffee Roasting
  • Coffee Grinding
  • Coffee Packaging
